# Cubo Marketing Plan

## Company Overview
**Cubo** - UK flexible workspace provider with 14 locations across Birmingham, London, Manchester, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Leeds, Newcastle, Nottingham, Derby, and Sheffield.

**Target Audiences:**
- SMEs & growing businesses
- Tech companies & startups
- Freelancers & remote workers
- Corporate teams needing flexible space

**Value Proposition:** Premium flexible workspaces with 24/7 access, multi-location flexibility, and community-focused culture.

## 3. Email Campaigns

### Campaign 1: Lead Nurture Sequence (Post-Enquiry)

**Goal:** Convert enquiries to booked tours
**Trigger:** Form submission / enquiry
**Sequence:** 5 emails over 14 days

| Email | Day | Subject Line | Content Focus |
|-------|-----|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | 0 | "Your Cubo guide is here" | Welcome + location overview + book tour CTA |
| 2 | 2 | "What [100+ members] wish they knew before joining" | Social proof, member testimonials |
| 3 | 5 | "Quick question about your workspace search" | Personal touch, address common concerns |
| 4 | 9 | "Your exclusive tour offer expires soon" | Urgency + incentive (free day pass) |
| 5 | 14 | "Still looking? Here's what's new at Cubo" | New features/locations, soft re-engagement |

### Campaign 2: Member Onboarding

**Goal:** Activate new members, reduce churn
**Trigger:** Membership start date
**Sequence:** 4 emails over 30 days

| Email | Day | Subject Line | Content Focus |
|-------|-----|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | 0 | "Welcome to Cubo - here's your starter guide" | Access info, app download, key contacts |
| 2 | 3 | "3 things to do in your first week" | Book meeting room, attend event, meet neighbours |
| 3 | 14 | "How's your first two weeks going?" | Feedback request + support offer |
| 4 | 30 | "You're officially a Cubo regular!" | Celebrate milestone, referral program intro |

### Campaign 3: Re-Engagement (Lapsed Leads)

**Goal:** Revive cold leads
**Trigger:** No activity for 60+ days
**Sequence:** 3 emails over 21 days

| Email | Day | Subject Line Options (A/B Test) |
|-------|-----|--------------------------------|
| 1 | 0 | A: "Things have changed at Cubo" / B: "We saved your spot" |
| 2 | 7 | A: "New location alert: [City]" / B: "What workspace seekers are asking in 2026" |
| 3 | 21 | A: "Last chance: Your exclusive offer" / B: "Should we close your file?" |

### Subject Line Bank (A/B Testing)

**Curiosity-Driven:**
- "The workspace mistake most startups make"
- "What your office says about your company"

**Benefit-Driven:**
- "Work from 14 locations with one membership"
- "Your commute, your choice"

**Urgency-Driven:**
- "Only 2 private offices left in Manchester"
- "48 hours: Free month offer ends"

**Personalized:**
- "[Name], your Birmingham workspace awaits"
- "For growing teams like [Company]"

## 5. Analytics & KPIs

### Marketing Funnel

```
AWARENESS          CONSIDERATION         DECISION           RETENTION
    |                    |                   |                  |
Website visits      Lead enquiries       Tour bookings      Member NPS
Social reach        Email subscribers    Tour-to-join %     Referral rate
Brand searches      Content downloads    New members        Churn rate
```

### Key Performance Indicators

| Stage | KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-------|-----|--------|-------------|
| **Awareness** | Monthly website sessions | +15% QoQ | Google Analytics |
| | Social media reach | +20% QoQ | Platform analytics |
| | Branded search volume | +10% QoQ | Search Console |
| **Consideration** | Lead enquiries | 500/month | CRM |
| | Email list growth | +200/month | Email platform |
| | Content engagement rate | >3% | Analytics |
| **Decision** | Tour booking rate | 40% of enquiries | CRM |
| | Tour-to-member conversion | 50% | CRM |
| | Cost per acquisition | <£150 | Marketing spend / new members |
| **Retention** | Member NPS | >50 | Survey |
| | Monthly churn rate | <5% | Membership data |
| | Referral rate | 15% of new members | CRM |

### Reporting Cadence

| Report | Frequency | Audience | Focus |
|--------|-----------|----------|-------|
| Marketing dashboard | Real-time | Marketing team | Live KPIs |
| Weekly performance | Weekly | Marketing + Sales | Leads, tours, content performance |
| Monthly deep-dive | Monthly | Leadership | Full funnel, channel analysis, recommendations |
| Quarterly review | Quarterly | Board/Exec | ROI, strategic insights, next quarter planning |

### Attribution Model

**Recommended:** Multi-touch attribution (linear or time-decay)

**Tracked touchpoints:**
- First touch (how they found Cubo)
- Content engaged (blogs, guides, videos)
- Email interactions
- Ad clicks
- Tour booking source
- Final conversion trigger

### Funnel Analysis Framework

**Enquiry → Tour:**
- Track: Days to book, follow-up touchpoints needed
- Optimize: Email nurture timing, CTA clarity

**Tour → Member:**
- Track: Objections raised, competitive mentions, decision timeline
- Optimize: Tour experience, follow-up process, offer structure

**Member → Advocate:**
- Track: NPS score, referral program participation, social mentions
- Optimize: Onboarding, community engagement, referral incentives
