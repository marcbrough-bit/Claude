# Analytics & Reporting Skill

## Overview
Analyze marketing performance data and generate actionable reports and dashboards.

## Commands

### generate-report
Create comprehensive marketing performance reports with insights and recommendations.

**Parameters:**
- `type` - Report type (campaign, channel, monthly, quarterly)
- `metrics` - Key metrics to include
- `format` - Output format (summary, detailed, executive)

### track-kpis
Define and monitor key performance indicators for marketing initiatives.

**Parameters:**
- `goals` - Business goals to track against
- `channels` - Marketing channels to monitor
- `frequency` - Reporting frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)

### analyze-funnel
Analyze conversion funnel performance and identify drop-off points and optimization opportunities.

**Parameters:**
- `stages` - Funnel stages to analyze
- `segment` - Audience segment to focus on
- `period` - Analysis time period
