# Content Strategy Skill

## Overview
Plan and develop content strategies aligned with business goals, target audiences, and brand voice.

## Commands

### analyze-audience
Analyze target audience demographics, interests, and behavior patterns to inform content decisions.

**Parameters:**
- `industry` - Target industry or niche
- `demographics` - Audience demographic details
- `goals` - Business objectives for the content

### generate-content-calendar
Create a structured content calendar with topics, formats, and publishing schedules.

**Parameters:**
- `duration` - Calendar timeframe (e.g., "30 days", "quarter")
- `channels` - Target distribution channels
- `frequency` - Publishing frequency per channel

### topic-research
Research trending topics and content gaps within a specific niche or industry.

**Parameters:**
- `niche` - Industry or topic area
- `competitors` - Competitor URLs or names for gap analysis
- `format` - Preferred content formats (blog, video, podcast, etc.)
