# Email Campaigns Skill

## Overview
Design and optimize email marketing campaigns with subject lines, templates, and A/B testing strategies.

## Commands

### draft-email
Create email copy for marketing campaigns with compelling content and clear CTAs.

**Parameters:**
- `purpose` - Email purpose (welcome, promotion, newsletter, re-engagement)
- `audience` - Target audience segment
- `tone` - Desired tone (formal, casual, urgent, friendly)

### generate-subject-lines
Generate A/B testable subject lines optimized for open rates.

**Parameters:**
- `topic` - Email topic or campaign theme
- `count` - Number of variations to generate
- `style` - Style preference (curiosity, urgency, benefit-driven, personalized)

### plan-drip-campaign
Design a multi-step automated email sequence for nurturing leads or onboarding users.

**Parameters:**
- `goal` - Campaign objective (nurture, onboard, re-engage, upsell)
- `steps` - Number of emails in the sequence
- `interval` - Time between emails
