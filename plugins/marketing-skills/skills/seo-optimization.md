# SEO Optimization Skill

## Overview
Optimize content for search engines with keyword research, on-page SEO, and technical recommendations.

## Commands

### keyword-research
Discover high-value keywords and phrases for targeting in content and campaigns.

**Parameters:**
- `seed_keywords` - Starting keywords or phrases
- `intent` - Search intent type (informational, transactional, navigational)
- `competition` - Desired competition level (low, medium, high)

### audit-seo
Perform an SEO audit on existing content or pages to identify improvement opportunities.

**Parameters:**
- `url` - Page URL or content to audit
- `focus_areas` - Specific areas to review (meta tags, headings, links, etc.)

### optimize-content
Rewrite or enhance content for better search engine visibility and ranking potential.

**Parameters:**
- `content` - The content to optimize
- `target_keywords` - Primary and secondary keywords
- `word_count` - Target word count range
